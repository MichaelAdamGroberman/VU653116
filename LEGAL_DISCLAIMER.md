# Legal Disclaimer

## Good-Faith Security Research

This repository documents the findings of good-faith security research conducted in accordance with coordinated vulnerability disclosure principles. All research was performed on hardware owned by the researcher and coordinated through federal agencies designated by the U.S. government for this purpose.

## Authorization and Coordination

- Research was coordinated through **CERT/CC** (Case VU#653116)
- CISA published [ICSA-26-055-03](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/ics-advisories/icsa-26-055-03) on **February 24, 2026**
- CISA published [ICSA-26-055-03 Update A](https://www.cisa.gov/news-events/ics-advisories/icsa-26-055-03) on **April 2, 2026**

## Scope of Testing

- All testing was performed exclusively on **researcher-owned hardware**
- No unauthorized access to vendor systems, third-party accounts, or other users' devices was performed
- No exploit code, automation scripts, or weaponized tools are published in this repository
- Actionable technical details have been redacted to reduce attacker enablement

## Purpose

This publication serves the public interest by:

1. Informing affected device owners of verified remediation status
2. Enabling informed risk decisions for ~138,000 registered device owners
3. Providing transparency by publishing the researcher's technical findings alongside the federal advisory
4. Documenting the coordinated disclosure process for the public record
